The Studio workspace serves as the multi-disciplinary creative and structural nucleus of the tupynambalucas.dev ecosystem. It functions as the master repository for brand strategy, high-fidelity design sources across professional suites, and a technical orchestration layer for our self-hosted design environment integrated with the Aide AI assistant.

This section covers:

- **Brand Identity**: The core design strategy, typography, color palette tokens, and standard asset variables.
- **Penpot & Aide**: Our self-hosted collaborative design platform integrated with the Aide AI assistant.
- **Studio & Creative Assets**: Version-controlled master files from professional design suites and dynamic Cloudflare R2 bucket synchronization.
